Golden Teacher (GT) is a widely popular and beginner-friendly strain of Psilocybe cubensis magic mushrooms. Named for its distinct golden-yellow caps and reputation for offering insightful experiences, it contains naturally occurring psychedelic compounds: psilocybin and psilocin.
The Penis Envy mushroom is a highly potent strain of the psychedelic fungus Psilocybe cubensis, famous for its phallic appearance and intense psychoactive effects. It is widely considered one of the strongest naturally occurring strains of magic mushrooms.
The Enigma mushroom is a rare, highly potent genetic mutation of the Psilocybe cubensis (typically derived from the Tidal Wave strain). Instead of growing traditional caps and stems, it develops in dense, brain-like blobs. Because it produces no spores, it must be propagated using Liquid Culture or cloned
The B+ (B-Plus) mushroom is a highly popular and resilient strain of Psilocybe cubensis. First developed in Florida in the 1990s, the name is a nod to its "big fruits." It is widely favored by researchers and mycology enthusiasts for its adaptability and robust growth.
